Submitting Package Tracking Numbers

After users have shipped their package to TGen North, they can submit a tracking number from the package summary page, so that TGen North is prepared for the packages arrival.


Users can click the "Enter Tracking Number" button directly on the summary page for the package they have shipped.


After clicking the "Enter Tracking Number" button, users are prompted to enter the tracking number.


After entering the tracking number and hitting "OK", users can see the tracking number that was successfully sent to TGen North.
