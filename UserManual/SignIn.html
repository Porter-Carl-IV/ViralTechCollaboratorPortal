Pimpoint Collaborator Portal Sign-in


The collaborator portal uses Google Authentication. In order to log into the website, users must have a Google account. Once you have a google account, users need to contact TGen north to be added to the Pimpoint database. Once users have a google account and have contacted TGen North to set up a user account, they can log into the website by clicking the "Sign In" button on the main page of the website.


Users can log out of the website at any time by clicking the "Sign Out" button at the top right of any page.
